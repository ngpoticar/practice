About Me

Hello, I'm Nathalie Grace P. Poticar, a self-taught artist. My artistic journey began during the challenging times of the 2019 pandemic when I discovered the therapeutic power of art.

Interestingly, creativity runs in my family, and my father, too, is good in art. Drawing inspiration from his talent, I embarked on my own artistic exploration, dedicating myself to of charcoal drawing.

My preferred medium, charcoal, allows me to capture the subtle nuances of light and shadow, creating evocative and expressive pieces.However, I still need to enhance my charcoal drawing skills to achieve a more realistic results. Additionally, I delve into the digital art,it was 2019 when I also started digital drawing, utilizing Clip Studio Paint to bring my ideas to life with a contemporary twist.

Beyond my personal artistic pursuits, I contribute to the creative landscape of our school as a cartoonist in the publication office. This role has not only refined my skills but also allowed me to connect with the vibrant artistic community within the school.

Join me on this artistic journey as I continue to explore the intersection of traditional and digital art, always seeking new ways to express my creativity and share my unique perspective with the world.
