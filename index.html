Hi!

I'm Nathalie Grace P. Poticar, an artist on a transformative journey fueled by passion and exploration. In the midst of the 2019 pandemic, I discovered the therapeutic nature of art, particularly drawn to the captivating world of charcoal. As I navigate between traditional and digital mediums, I actively contribute to my school's creative community as a cartoonist. Looking ahead, my ambition is set on taking on commissions and sharing my unique perspective with a broader audience, reflecting not just technical skill but a profound love for storytelling through visual art. Join me on this exciting journey of growth and expression!
